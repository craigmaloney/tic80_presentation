Why TIC80?
==========

* Open Source (MIT license)
* Comparable to 16 bit consoles
* Integrated Development Environment
* LUA Based
* Closest feature-wise to PICO-8

----

Specifications
==============

* 64K RAM
* 16 Color Sprites
* Map Editor
* Exportable to HTML5 pages
* Bank Switching (Pro mode)
* Number of Simultaneous Sounds
